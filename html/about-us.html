About us

Aurion.

Aurion Creative is a Pretoria-based AI agency built for founders, startups, and businesses that want to grow fast without drowning in manual work. We are where engineering meets practicality: ideas become working systems that answer your customers, capture your leads, and take repetitive admin off your plate.

Your vision becomes our blueprint. Whether it's an AI chatbot that works around the clock, a custom software tool like our BOQ automation platform for quantity surveyors, or a website built to convert, we turn bold concepts into real solutions that change how you work, communicate, and scale.

We focus on three things and do them properly: AI automation and chatbots (our flagship service), web design and development, and custom software. Every project starts with your actual workflow, not a template of ours, and ends with something your team uses every day. With each build, we push boundaries, refine experiences, and craft solutions that move businesses forward.
